0°-Renderings of All Presented Stimuli

During the listening experiment, all stimuli (excpet the stereo anchor) were presented as head-tracked (horizontal axis) binaural streams using the SoundScape Renderer (SSR) in Binaural Playback Mode (BPB). The streams were rendered in 1° steps from -45° to 45°. The following audio files contain the 0°-renderings of all presented stimuli to get a quick impression of how they sounded.
